How We Met

From simple moments to forever. Sometimes the best things in life come when you’re not looking for them. What began as a simple connection quietly grew into something neither of them could imagine living without.

They met at work and, without even realizing it, began choosing each other more and more. Hanging out became a daily thing — inventing plans, laughing endlessly, and spending long afternoons at Keren’s parents’ home. It was there that they discovered how effortlessly they connected, bonding over board games, LEGOs, and the simple joy of being together.

Their first official date took them to New Orleans for a Matt Rife comedy show. They spent hours walking through Bourbon Street, talking about everything and nothing at all. When they were together, the world seemed to fade away — just the two of them, fully present and completely understood.
