Other Details You’ll Want to Know

Our wedding attire is formal / elegant. Click “Dress Code” above to view visual inspiration. Think fall colors for inspiration. We kindly ask guests to avoid wearing white, burgundy, beige or red, as these colors are part of our wedding palette.

Photos During Ceremony

Yes! Guests are welcome to take photos. We simply ask that everyone remains seated and avoids stepping into the aisle or in front of the photographers, so they can capture our special moments.

Plus-Ones

Due to space limitations, only guests listed on the invitation are able to attend. If a plus-one has been reserved for you, it will be indicated on your invitation. Thank you for understanding.

Children

Of course—we love the little ones! We simply ask parents to help keep the church ceremony quiet and ensure children remain seated during special moments so everyone can enjoy the ceremony and our photographers can capture the memories.
